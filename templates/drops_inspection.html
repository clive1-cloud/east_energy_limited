Eliminating Life-Threatening Hazards from Dropped Objects

What Is DROPS Inspection?

**DROPS**—short for **Dropped Object Prevention Scheme**—is a systematic program designed to identify, assess, and eliminate risks associated with dropped objects. At Energy East Atlantic, our DROPS Inspection process includes:

  • Management System Review: A detailed evaluation of your dropped-object prevention protocols, including load test certificates, incident logs, and maintenance records.
  • Physical Inspection: A thorough visual examination of the derrick, substructure, and all mounted equipment to detect loose fittings, unsecured items, and structural vulnerabilities.
  • Expert Recommendations: Based on inspection findings, our specialists provide actionable guidance to strengthen safety measures and reduce exposure to dropped-object hazards.

Why DROPS Inspection Matters

The importance of DROPS Inspection cannot be overstated. It plays a critical role in preventing workplace accidents and protecting lives. Key risks addressed through this program include:

  • Loose or unsecured tools and equipment
  • Improperly stacked materials
  • Unguarded openings and elevated platforms
  • Unsafe work practices and procedural gaps

How Energy East Atlantic Conducts DROPS Inspection

Our DROPS Inspection services follow a structured, multi-phase approach:

  • Documentation Audit: We assess all relevant safety documentation to understand current practices and identify gaps.
  • On-Site Visual Inspection: Our certified inspectors examine high-risk areas, including derricks and substructures, to detect potential hazards.
  • Safety Enhancement Plan: We deliver a comprehensive report with prioritized recommendations to improve safety and prevent future incidents.

Benefits of DROPS Inspection

Partnering with Energy East Atlantic for DROPS Inspection offers tangible advantages:

  • **Reduced Risk of Incidents:** Proactive hazard identification minimizes the likelihood of dropped-object accidents.
  • **Strengthened Safety Culture:** Our inspections reinforce a safety-first mindset across your workforce.
  • **Regulatory Compliance:** We help ensure your operations meet industry standards and avoid costly penalties.
  • **Lower Liability Exposure:** Documented inspections and corrective actions demonstrate due diligence, protecting your organization’s reputation and financial stability.
